Biography
Her name is Borjana Donceva, and she lived in a small town called Radovish in North Macedonia. Currently she is a student at “Ss. Cyril and Methodius” University that is located in Skopje. She was born on 26th of March year 2002, in Shtip, North Macedonia. Her grandparents are the ones who served as babysitter since her parents weren’t home for work.
When she was six years old her parents enrolled her in Kindergarten then to Elementary. She was only seven years old when she was on the first grade. Her elementary days were incredibly awesome. She gained many friends, achieved honors and awards. In 2017, she graduated from elementary as the best student of the generation.
Then she became a high-school-freshman in Orce Nikolov High School in Skopje, where her mother also started working. During the working days they were in Skopje and they spent the weekend with her father and brother in their home in Radovish. Her favourite subjects during school were maths and IT.
In 2021, she graduated from high school and she decided to apply to “Ss. Cyril and Methodius” University that is located in Skopje. She was so happy when she got accepted and she was so grateful because she was going to study what she was always wanted.
Now, she is a college-freshman in Ss. Cyril and Methodius” University in Skopje.
